Executive Search

Executive Search – Our Executive Search Practice is driven by seasoned experts who leave no stone unturned to pick the best leadership talents for your firm. Senior leadership hiring can make an impressionable impact on the way your business is run, people strategy is influenced and future complexities are viewed.

That’s why you need robust, flexible and game-changing leaders who deliver with both purpose and passion.

Onsite Recruitment Desks

Onsite Recruitment Desks – Our onsite recruitment desks are designed to fill in as your core HR department, optimally meeting all your manpower requirements by mapping the best talents from the labour market.

Candidate Lifecycle Management

Candidate Lifecycle Management – From onboarding to talent management to offboarding, our end-to-end candidate lifecycle management system ensures that your multi-generational and diversified workforce benefits from a hassle-free, experiential journey.

Recruitment Process outsourcing

Recruitment Process Outsourcing – Leverage our rich competency in recruitment process outsourcing by leaving recruitment paperwork with us. We oversee administration, induction, system & tools handover, office ID access and initial training.

Turnkey Recruitment

Turnkey Recruitment – Creating the best, deliverable and reliable hires for mission-critical roles and turnkey projects is also our forte.

We mobilize comprehensive personnel resources for time-bound projects within your expenses to facilitate seamless workflow.
